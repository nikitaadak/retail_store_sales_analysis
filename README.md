# retail_store_sales_analysis
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to identify high-performing product categories, profitable customer segments, and store performance trends. The goal was to support business decision-making using data-driven insights.
---

##  Business Problem Statement

The retail company wanted to understand:

* Which product categories generate the highest profit?
* Which customer segment contributes the most revenue?
* Are there any seasonal sales fluctuations?
* Which store locations are underperforming?
* How can overall profitability be improved?

The objective was to analyze sales and profit data and provide actionable recommendations to improve business performance.

---

##  Business Requirements

The business required:

* Overall Sales & Profit tracking
* Profit Margin analysis by category
* Monthly Sales trend analysis
* Store location performance comparison
* Customer segmentation analysis (Age & Spending Behavior)
* Identification of growth opportunities

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset included the following fields:

* Sales Amount
* Profit Amount
* Product Category
* Store Location
* Customer Age Group
* Spending Segment
* Order Date (Month & Year)

---

##  Data Cleaning & Transformation

* Removed duplicate records
* Handled missing values
* Created calculated measure for *Profit Margin %*
* Created measures for Total Sales and Total Profit
* Structured date column for monthly trend analysis

Tools Used: Power BI, Excel, SQL

---

##  KPIs Developed

* Total Sales: ₹533K+
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin: 8.29%
* Sales by Category
* Sales by Store Location
* Customer Count by Segment
* Monthly Sales Trend

---

##  Key Insights

* Overall profit margin is 8.29%, indicating stable profitability.
* Health & Beauty is the highest profit margin category.
* Middle-age customers contribute the highest revenue.
* Sales peak in March & May, while February & July show dips.
* Lokhandwala & Powai are top-performing store locations.

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Promote high-margin categories to improve overall profitability.
* Launch targeted campaigns for middle-age customers.
* Introduce seasonal offers during low-sales months.
* Improve strategy for low-performing store locations.
* Develop loyalty programs to increase repeat purchases.

---

##  Conclusion

The analysis helped identify high-performing categories, core customer segments, and seasonal sales trends. Based on the findings, strategic marketing and operational improvements can enhance profitability and revenue growth.
